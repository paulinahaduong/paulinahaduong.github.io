Yoga Teaching

I am a RYT-200 yoga teacher trained in O2-style yoga, developed by Mimi Loureiro. O2 Yoga is an Astanga-based vinyasa flow focused on creative sequencing and alignment, and all poses are taught progressively. Though I can usually be found at O2 Yoga (Cambridge/Somerville), I am also available to teach private and corporate classes.
