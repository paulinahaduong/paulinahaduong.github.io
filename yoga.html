yoga teaching

I am on the Ashtanga Faculty at Down Under, teaching Ashtanga Flow at Harvard. All poses are taught progressively, with appropriate modifications for injury and accessibility. I believe that yoga can be accessible to every body and am committed to creating a welcoming environment for queer and trans people of color. I bring a constructionist and trauma-informed lens to helping students develop body awareness and mindfulness. Available for private, studio, and corporate classes.
